How we started

The Chicane Racing India Team was initiated on 26thNovember2016 and the unit was
formed on 21st of March 2017 .The Team was registered as an official team on April10th
2017.

Prime Objective

The prime objective of the team is to train individuals in industrial development and to
polish the talent present in each individuals.It is also our objective to design and fabricate a
businessmodel of a performance bike in light-weight category and fabricate the same to
compete in the fifth edition of the Moto Student event held in Spain

Team

The team current  consists of 6 domains including corporate with a total strength of
approximately 50 students led by Mr.J.DanialStephen, thefacultyadvisor. 

Moto Student 

Moto Student is an international competition ,promoted by the Moto Engineering Foundation.
Student teams from various universities face the challenge of designing and developing the
prototype of a racing motorcycle ,similar to those used in the Moto3 WorldChampionship
class
